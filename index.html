Our Services

  • Exterior Hand Wash & Wax: Meticulous wash using pH-balanced products, followed by a hand-applied wax for a deep, protective shine.
  • Bug & Tar Removal: Effective cleaning of stubborn road grime, insects, and tar without damaging paint.
  • Wheel & Tire Detailing: Deep clean and dress wheels, tires, and wheel wells for a crisp, finished look.
  • Interior Vacuum & Steam Clean: Thorough vacuuming and steam disinfection to eliminate debris, stains, and bacteria.
  • Upholstery Shampoo: Deep cleaning of fabric or leather seats to remove embedded dirt and restore comfort.
  • Plastic & Trim Conditioning: Revitalizes dull trim and protects against fading and cracking.

About Us

Southern Nevada Detail Co. is a locally owned and operated mobile auto detailing service based in Henderson, Nevada. We deliver professional-grade interior and exterior detailing to your home, office, or wherever it’s most convenient for you. With high-end products and passion for perfection, we bring out the best in every vehicle we touch.
